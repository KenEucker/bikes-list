Area

Most events are in the Portland area (south of the Columbia). These events have black titles in the weekly grid, and have no location icon in the detailed listings. For most of these events, clicking on the address will take you to a bike map showing its location.
Some events are in the Vancouver area (north of the Columbia). These events have blue titles in the weekly grid, and have a blue Washington icon in the left margin of the detailed listings.

To close this window, click anywhere on it.
