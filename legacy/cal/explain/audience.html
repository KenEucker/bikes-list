Audience

Family-Friendly events are those where parents are specifically asked to bring their children. In the weekly grid at the top of the calendar page, these events are marked with green times. In the detailed event listings, they are marked with a tricycle icon in the left margin.
Adult only (21+) events are those where children are specifically not allowed, usually because the event takes place in a bar. In the weekly grid, these events are marked with red times. In the detailed event listings, they are marked with a beer icon in the left margin.
Most events are General audience events. Children are allowed, but they may be bored or they may not have enough stamina to enjoy the event. If you have any questions, contact the event organizer. (Contact information is listed at the bottom of each event in the detailed listings. If no other contact method is offered, use the event's "" button.) General audience events have plain black times in the weekly grid, and have no icon in the detailed listings.
