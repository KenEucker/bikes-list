Group Accounts

Anybody can add an event to the calendar; you don't need an account to do that.

However, if you expect to add multiple events then there are some real advantages to establishing an account:

  • Easier to add new events. Your contact information and default image are pre-filled on the event entry form.
  • Easier to add a repeat event. If a new event is similar to a previous event you did, you can copy the old event to a new date and then edit it.
  • Easier to edit events. When you log in, you can see all of your events at once, and edit any of them just by clicking on it.
  • Easier to promote your events within your group. You can embed a calendar into your own web page, showing just your events, in a color theme that you choose.
