Public health update: The Oregon Health Authority is responding to an outbreak of respiratory illness, called COVID-19, caused by a novel (new) coronavirus. Health officials urge social distancing, good hand hygiene, covering coughs, and staying home if you are sick. For additional information and resources, see the Multnomah County COVID-19 FAQ.

To stem the spread of COVID-19 in Oregon, Executive Order 20-12 prohibits all non-essential social and recreational gatherings of individuals, regardless of size. Until further notice, all events on or after March 23, 2020 are cancelled. For more info on calendar updates and tips on riding safely in these times, check out our Public Health page. Stay safe, stay healthy!
