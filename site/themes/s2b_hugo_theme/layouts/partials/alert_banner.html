Regarding the COVID-19 pandemic: The Oregon Health Authority is responding to an outbreak of respiratory illness, called COVID-19, caused by a novel (new) coronavirus. Health officials urge social distancing, good hand hygiene, covering coughs, and staying home if you are sick. Group gatherings are strongly discouraged.

Many events are being cancelled or postponed, even if they appear active here. We recommend contacting the event organizer for confirmation and further details.
